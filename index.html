❓ What is a Dividend?

A dividend is simply a slice of the bakery's net profits paid back out to the owners as a thank-you reward for investing capital.

❓ What does Winding Up Mean?

Winding up means closing the business down completely, selling all properties/ovens, and distributing the cash back down the structural priority ladder.
